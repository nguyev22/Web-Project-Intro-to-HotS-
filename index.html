What is Heroes of the Storm and how does it differ from the popular titles?

  • A free-to-play Multiplayer Online Battle Area (aka MOBA)
  • Two teams of 5 players fighting against each other to destroy the opponent's "castle"
  • Multiples maps that alternate
  • Average game time of 20 mins (Short games)
  • Doesn't involve item optimization
  • Don't have to worry about farming gold
  • Lots and lots of fighting
